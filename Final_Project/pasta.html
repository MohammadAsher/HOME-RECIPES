Lasagna Primavera

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles: 12 sheets
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Assorted vegetables (such as bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms,carrots): 3 cups, chopped
  • Marinara sauce: 3 cups
  • Ricotta cheese: 2 cups
  • Mozzarella cheese: 2 cups, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese: ½ cup, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves: Chopped, for garnish (optional)

    • Preparation

    Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add chopped vegetables to the skillet and cook until tender. Season with salt and pepper. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese over the noodles, followed by a layer of cooked vegetables. Repeat the layers of noodles, sauce, ricotta cheese, and vegetables until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of sauce on top. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the top layer. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped fresh basil leaves if desired.

Pesto Cavatappi

Ingredients

  • Cavatappi pasta: 8 oz
  • Pesto sauce: 1 cup
  • Cherry tomatoes: 1 cup, halved
  • Pine nuts: ¼ cup, toasted
  • Parmesan cheese: ½ cup, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves: Chopped, for garnish (optional)

Preparation

Cook cavatappi pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. In a large skillet, heat pesto sauce over medium heat until warmed through. Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat evenly with the pesto sauce. Stir in halved cherry tomatoes and toasted pine nuts. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh basil leaves if desired.

Pappardelle with Wild Mushroom Ragout

Ingredients

  • Pappardelle pasta: 8 oz
  • Mixed wild mushrooms (such as shiitake, oyster, cremini): 1 lb, sliced
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Shallots: 2, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Dry white wine: ½ cup
  • Heavy cream: ½ cup
  • Fresh thyme leaves: 1 tbsp
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese: ½ cup, grated
  • Fresh parsley: Chopped, for garnish (optional)

Preparation

Cook pappardelle pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped shallots and minced garlic, and sauté until softened. Add sliced wild mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they release their moisture and become golden brown. Deglaze the skillet with dry white wine and simmer until the wine is reduced by half. Stir in heavy cream and fresh thyme leaves. Simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens slightly. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add cooked pappardelle pasta to the skillet and toss to coat evenly with the mushroom ragout. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley if desired.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti: 8 oz
  • Bacon or pancetta: 4 slices, diced
  • Eggs: 2
  • Parmesan cheese: ½ cup, grated
  • Black pepper: 1 tsp, freshly ground
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced (optional)

Preparation

Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. In a skillet, cook diced bacon or pancetta until crispy. Remove from skillet and set aside. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. In the same skillet, heat olive oil. Add minced garlic if using and sauté until fragrant. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat with the olive oil. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing until the eggs thicken but do not scramble. Add the crispy bacon or pancetta back to the skillet and toss to combine. Serve immediately, garnished with additional grated Parmesan cheese and black pepper if desired.

Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta: 8 oz
  • Heavy cream: 1 cup
  • Butter: 4 tbsp
  • Parmesan cheese: 1 cup, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parsley: Chopped, for garnish (optional)

Preparation

Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Gradually add grated Parmesan cheese to the cream mixture, stirring constantly until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth. Season the Alfredo sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Add the cooked fettuccine to the saucepan and toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce. Serve immediately, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.

Penne Arrabbiata

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta: 8 oz
  • Olive oil: 3 tbsp
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Red pepper flakes: 1-2 tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Crushed tomatoes: 1 can (14 oz)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves: Chopped, for garnish (optional)

Preparation

Cook penne pasta according to package instructions until al dente. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté until fragrant. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Season the Arrabbiata sauce with salt to taste. Add the cooked penne to the skillet and toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh basil leaves if desired.
